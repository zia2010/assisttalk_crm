# AssistTalk Labs — Mission, Goals & Outcome Targets

> Last Updated: March 12, 2026

---

## Mission Statement

To empower small service-based businesses — starting with real estate agents — with AI-powered automation that ensures they never miss a lead, never forget a follow-up, and always look professional. We replace missed calls with closed deals.

---

## Vision

Become the go-to AI automation partner for independent real estate agents, delivering enterprise-level lead capture and client management at a price any solo agent can afford.

---

## Core Values

1. **Reliability over features** — A system that works 100% of the time beats one with 20 features that breaks
2. **Client outcomes first** — If the agent isn't closing more deals, we've failed
3. **Simplicity** — The agent should never need a tutorial
4. **Transparency** — Honest pricing, honest results, no lock-in tricks

---

## Success Criteria

### Client #1 is a SUCCESS if:
- [ ] AI receptionist answers 95%+ calls correctly
- [ ] Agent gets SMS notification within 10 seconds of every call
- [ ] At least 5 leads captured in first 2 weeks
- [ ] Agent says "this is saving me time" unprompted
- [ ] Agent pays $500 after trial without hesitation

### The BUSINESS is a SUCCESS at 6 months if:
- [ ] 4 clients paying $500/mo = $2,000 MRR
- [ ] All 4 retained (0 churn)
- [ ] Weekly time commitment ≤ 7 hours
- [ ] System runs with minimal intervention
- [ ] Full-time job is NOT affected

## Anti-Goals (What We Will NOT Do)

| Anti-Goal | Why |
|---|---|
| Take more than 4 clients in Phase 1 | Protect quality, avoid burnout |
| Do outreach after 4 clients are locked | Focus on retention, not growth |
| Build features nobody asked for | Only build what gets/keeps clients |
| Compete on price | $500 is fair — never discount below this after launch |
| Sacrifice sleep for deadlines | Sustainable pace > heroic sprints |

---

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI receptionist gives wrong info | Medium | High | Strict prompt engineering, property data validation, human fallback |
| Agent churns after trial | Medium | Medium | Deliver "wow moment" in week 1, build switching cost via data |
| Vapi/Twilio outage | Low | High | Monitor uptime, have fallback to direct call forwarding |
| Full-time job affected | Low | Critical | Hard cap at 4 hrs/night weekdays, Sundays always OFF |
| No prospects respond | Medium | Medium | Iterate on email copy, try DMs + cold calls, leverage LinkedIn |

---

## Personal Guardrails

- **Sunday = OFF.** No exceptions.
- **Friday night = prospecting only** (until 4 clients locked, then stop).
- **Max 4 hrs/night** on weekdays (no 2 AM sessions).
- **1 client at a time** until first 2 convert, then 2 at a time.
- **Health first.** If tired, sleep. The business can wait a day.
